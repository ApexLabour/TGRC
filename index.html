About Us

The Glass Resorting Company is fully B-BBEE (Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment) comliant and approved. We are also fully tax compliant; We hold a clearance certificate providing written confirmation from SARS that our tax affairs are in order at the date of issue of the certificate.

About Us

The Glass Resorting Company (PTY) LTD- (TGRC) was founded by Devan Naidoo after over 15 years experience Supply Chain Management. With a passion for the glass industry, noticing the lack of skilled labour and retention plans within the industry.

  • Skilled labour for Industry

    Since it's inception, TGRC has primarily sourced and trained labour for the glass industry. We have recently diversified our Recruitment Sectors and now supply qualified and highly skilled staff to a variety of industries within the South African market.

  • MEMBER OF THE NBCRFLI

    Due to a large client base in the Logistics Industry, The Glass Resorting Company has affiliated itself with and become a member of the NBCRFLI – National Bargaining Council for The Road Freight and Logistics Industry since 2015.

  • Some of our major clients include:

    CONSOL, Imperial Logistics, RABS Logistics, Overnight Logistics, Nampak, and Halewood Breweries, and many more.

Our Services

Our ServiceS

At The Glass Resorting Company we provide a wide range of fully trained and qualified, highly skilled labour solutions.

Cross Packers and Resorters

Identify and remove bottles with specific defects such as: Danny Cracks, Finish Cracks and Under Ring Cracks, Vertical Cracks (split finish), Off Plumb, Drop Bases, Ovalities. Cross packers can also be used during breakdowns online.

Final Checkers

Are trained and qualified to identify and rectify: Burn Holes, Missing Units, Loose Shrouds – Cold Shroud (pallets kick when cold shrouded), Foreign Objects or Broken Glass. Providing essential quality control and inspections.

Light Screen Viewers (LSV)

Can be used on QC lines during difficult runs for tapered, jars, flash, or square bottles. LSVs can be deployed to customer’s sites during initial runs ensuring success, as well as eliminating suspected defective production problems on customer lines and can clear up breakages.

Specialist Areas

We specialize in supplying visual inspectors, faint stippling and dirty ware, and specifically: Forming Specialists, Forming Managers, Forehearth and Feeder Specialists, Furnace Experts, Glass Fracture Analysis Experts, Glass and Glass Condition Experts

Specialist Drivers

We source and train specialist, fully licensed drivers, with good driving records to be able to handle operating forklifts up to 3.5 tons, double forks, large and small trucks with codes 10 and 14 licenses, as required, as well as VGAs

Staffing Options Available

Drivers, pickers, packers, inspectors, riggers, machine operators, product and quality control checkers, electricians, welders, administrators, store clerks, store managers, receiving and dispatch assistants, and many more.
